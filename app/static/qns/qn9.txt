                                                    Aliquot Sum

Given a positive integer n, Print its aliquot sum. 
The aliquot sum of a number is the sum of all its proper divisors, that is, all divisors of n excluding n itself. 

CONSTRAINT:
    1 ​≤ N ​≤ 1000

EXAMPLE:
    INPUT  :  6
    OUTPUT :  6

    INPUT  :  5
    OUTPUT :  1
