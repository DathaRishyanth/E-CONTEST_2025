                                                    Let’s Get Common (GCD Edition)

You are given two natural numbers A and B. Your task is to print the Greatest Common Divisor (GCD) of these 
two numbers.

CONSTRAINT:
	1 ≤ A,B ≤ 10^3

EXAMPLE:
	INPUT    :    4
				  10
	OUTPUT   :    2

	INPUT    :    7
				  9
	OUTPUT   :    1
